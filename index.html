      How to play

      • 🎮 Modes — one in each corner of the screen. Tap to switch.
      • 👆 Play — poke him for points; fast chains multiply your score. Your tools live at the bottom: weapon shop, mud, sponge, mic.
      • 👊 Combos — land secret poke sequences for MEGA points.
      • 🥁 Jam — he's the drum. Head = tak, belly = DUNG, legs = tek.
      • 🎯 Bonk! — whack the targets before they move. 30 seconds.
      • 🫧 Wash — scrub him clean before the falling trash wins.
      • 🏏 Weapons — spend ⭐ in the shop; 5 to collect, one is legendary.
      • 🎤 Mic — turn it on and he'll repeat what you say.
